# Privacy Policy — Road to President

**Last updated: 2026-04-28**

This Privacy Policy describes how "Road to President" ("we", "our", or "the app")
handles your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**Road to President does not collect any personally identifiable information.**

The app operates entirely offline on your device. Your game progress is saved
locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

- Your character's name, career progress, and game state
- Settings (audio on/off, language preference)
- Achievement progress

This data is stored in your device's local app storage and is automatically
deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob (if enabled)

If ad support is enabled in a future version, the app may use Google AdMob
to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information including:

- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- Approximate location (based on IP address)
- Device information (operating system, model)

This information is used by Google to serve relevant ads. You can review
Google's privacy practices at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under
"Google" > "Ads" > "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

### Google Play Services

When you install the app from the Google Play Store, Google collects certain
information as described in their privacy policy.

## Children's Privacy

Road to President is rated 17+ and is not directed at children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, the security of that
data depends on your device's own security measures.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted
in the app and on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

## How to Host This Policy (Required for Play Store)

Google Play requires a publicly accessible URL for your privacy policy.
Free options:

1. **GitHub Pages** (easiest if you have a GitHub account)
   - Create a public repo: `yourusername.github.io`
   - Upload this file as `privacy-policy.md`
   - Enable Pages in repo settings
   - URL becomes: `https://yourusername.github.io/privacy-policy`

2. **Google Sites** (drag-and-drop, free)
   - Go to sites.google.com
   - Create a new site, paste this content
   - Publish and copy the URL

3. **Notion** (free public pages)
   - Create a public Notion page
   - Share the URL

Any of these URLs can be used in the Google Play Console privacy policy field.
